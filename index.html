Pressing the setup button on the back side of NeTV brings up this setup help page.

For help on NeTV, please visit http://wiki.chumby.com/index.php/Netv

To reconfigure wifi by restoring factory settings:

1. Tap the setup button once
2. Immediately press and hold the setup button until the unit reboots.

The unit will reboot within four seconds of holding down the switch if the initial tap was registered. Do not remove power from the unit while it restarts.
